Real House Price Index - Global

The Real House Price Index is given by the ratio of the nominal house price index to the consumers' expenditure deflator in each country from the OECD national accounts database. Using this, it is evident that which countries have an overheated housing market such as China, Canada, and the US. Australia's property market is relatively stable in comparison to others.

House Prices - Australia

Here, we have the median house pricing in Australian capital cities, from 2005 to 2021. We can see that Sydney tend to have the highest median house pricing in terms of Australian cities followed by Melbourne. Meanwhile, mainland Australia's east cost seems to have the highest overall pricing, possibly due to population.
